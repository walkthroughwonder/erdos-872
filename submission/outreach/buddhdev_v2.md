## The reachability question

I tried to prove that isolated unit K_5 q-fibers cannot be generated by the T2 activation phase. I could not, but I also could not construct a full reachable instance. The obstruction is in Proposition A.6: Prolonger chooses "a safe edge supplied by that hypothesis," which is existential. Definition 4.5 does not specify:

1. a canonical safe-edge selector,
2. a selector chosen to make A.9 work,
3. every selector that always chooses a safe edge, or
4. every legal activation play.

These readings give different answers. Your Proposition A.2 already flags this distinction (some-legal-play vs. specific-Prolonger-strategy reachability) for the K_4 case.

What I can show is a conditional incidence witness. For n = 10^20, δ = 1/8, small primes {101, 103, 107, 109, 113}, and q = 4806305873305829, all ten targets p_i p_j q lie in (n/2, n] and form the K_5 slot incidence, and a legal partial play sequence (Prolonger secures the ten pairs, Shortener replies with harmless prime singletons) leaves all ten p_i p_j q targets live. Two independent scripts verify the interval conditions, legality, survival, and slot incidence. Details: [docs/t2_reachability_proof.md](https://github.com/walkthroughwonder/erdos-872/blob/main/docs/t2_reachability_proof.md).

The missing step for full reachability is certifying that those ten pair choices are safe edges in the full activation graph at the moments they are chosen, and that activation completes without attaching extra residual q-edges to the component.

## What I am asking

1. Is reachability in Definition 4.5 intended to be under a specific selector, or under all legal selectors that choose a safe edge?
2. Is there an unstated K_5-free (or bounded-rank, bounded-degree, or sparsity) invariant for residual q-fibers generated by the T2 activation strategy? If yes, A.3 holds as stated and my obstruction is consistent with the framework; the K_5 fiber simply never appears.
3. If no such invariant is intended, does Proposition A.9 need an additional hypothesis, or is there a global compensation across fibers I am missing?
